# Welcome to Sphere2Pano This tool converts the live feed of a camera from a spherical image to a panorama. ## Aim the camera Once your camera is plugged in, [open the live feed](live.html) and aim the camera at mirrored-sphere. Try to make the sphere as big as possible, like in the image below. ![Spherical image](sample.jpg) ## Calibration First, [you need to calibrate the source](calibrate.html). ## View Once calibaration is complete, [you can access the stream](stream.html).
